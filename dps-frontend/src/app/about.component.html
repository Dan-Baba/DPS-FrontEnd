Who are we?
We are Dynamic Portal Systems. Our team is comprised of only the brighest and most forward-thinking minds in the world of software engineering.
What we do
Our goal is to improve the quality of throughput in non-profit models by creating asynchronous, portal-based environments which are stable and secure. We provide a generic product to act as a framework for any non-profit group to go paperless while making the transition as easy as possible.
